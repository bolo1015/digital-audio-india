Radio & Legacy Broadcasters.

Guiding comprehensive digital transformation, mobile app deployment, and the modernization of archaic broadcast workflows.

The Pivot to Digital-First Audio.

Traditional airwaves are facing an existential shift. Broadcasters sit on decades of invaluable archives and massive live audiences, yet often lack the technical infrastructure to monetize them in the digital age. We serve as the architects for legacy media conglomerates, building the bridge from analog transmitters to highly profitable mobile streaming and on-demand ecosystems.

Departmental Transformation.

Establishing digital-first departments from the ground up. We guide the organizational change management required to shift legacy radio operations into agile tech-media workflows.

Streaming & Mobile App Deployment.

End-to-end strategic oversight for launching dedicated mobile streaming applications, transforming terrestrial listeners into targetable, logged-in digital users.

Simulcast Programmatic Yield.

Modernizing the ad break. We implement server-side ad replacement for live digital streams, allowing broadcasters to inject targeted programmatic audio ads over traditional terrestrial broadcast spots.

Proven Institutional Change.

We have successfully led the international expansion of major radio divisions and orchestrated the transition of legacy broadcasters—including RTM and Radio Mirchi—onto advanced digital streaming infrastructure.
